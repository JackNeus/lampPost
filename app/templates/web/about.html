The Problem

It can be hard to make sense of the overload of information about events on Princeton's campus. With hundreds of posters, listserv emails, and Facebook events, it can be hard to find the events you are interested in, or remember about them when the time comes.

The Solution

  • With lampPost, you have access to a searchable repository of all the events happening around Princeton.
  • All showings have buttons to export the time to your personal Google Calendar, so you can find them later.
  • Look at trending events, determined by those favorited by users.
  • If your event isn't included, fill out a quick form to immediately add it!
